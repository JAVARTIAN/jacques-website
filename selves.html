Selves (2010-2024)

These early paintings explored identity as a shifting container, where emotional states leaked through compressed bodily forms and distorted portraits. The self appeared not as a whole, but as a site of rupture—blurring inside and outside, surface and sensation. These were my first studies in what would later become a sustained investigation into form and composition.
